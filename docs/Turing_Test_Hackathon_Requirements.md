
# Turing Test Hackathon 2026 Requirements

## **Overview**
The **Turing Test Hackathon 2026** offers a **$100,000 USD prize pool** and focuses on **AI-driven blockchain applications**. Participants are encouraged to build innovative solutions that combine **AI**, **blockchain**, and **DeFi protocols**.

- **Submission Start**: 2026/05/01 03:00
- **Submission Deadline**: 2026/06/15 18:59

## **Tracks and Requirements**

### **AI Trading & Strategy**
- **AI Trading Strategy**:  
   - Develop **AI quant bots** and **macro-driven smart contracts** for automated trading strategies. Use **Python** and **Solidity templates**, integrate **Bybit API** for data feeds.

### **AI Alpha & Data**
- **Smart Money Tracking and Anomaly Detection**:
   - Build bots that detect **on-chain anomalies** and track smart money on **Telegram and Discord**.

### **AI x RWA (Real-World Assets)**
- **Dynamic Yield Strategies**:  
   - Use **Mantle’s RWA infrastructure** to build automated strategies for managing **assets** like **USDY** and **mETH**.

### **Consumer & Viral DApps**
- **Gamified Trading Interfaces**:  
   - Develop **shareable consumer applications** with **gamified** experiences, engaging users in trading and investing.

### **AI DevTools**
- **Smart Gas Optimization Tools**:  
   - Build **Mantle-specific audit assistants** and optimize **gas costs** for **DeFi transactions**.

### **Agentic Wallets & Economy**
- **Agentic Wallet Ecosystems**:
   - Use the **Byreal Skills CLI** to create **economies** around **agentic wallets**, with decentralized control.

## **Judging Criteria**
1. **Technical Depth (30%)**:
   - Demonstrating **robustness** and **efficiency** of the AI systems and smart contracts.

2. **Innovation (25%)**:
   - Bringing **new ideas** to Web3, **AI**, and **DeFi** that drive forward the industry.

3. **Mantle Ecosystem Contribution (25%)**:
   - Integrating seamlessly with **Mantle Network**, showing long-term utility and impact.

4. **Product Completeness (20%)**:
   - A **complete working demo**, a **functional user interface**, and **well-documented code**.

## **Submission Requirements**

- **GitHub Repository**:  
  Submit your **open-source code** on GitHub with clear **documentation**.

- **Live Demo**:  
  Provide a **live demo link** hosted on **Vercel**, **Heroku**, or another cloud platform.

- **Demo Video**:  
  A **2-3 minute demo video** showing how the platform works, including AI agent performance, integration with **Mantle Network**, and transparency features.

- **Smart Contracts**:  
  Submit your **smart contracts** deployed on the **Mantle Mainnet**.

- **On-Chain Verification**:
