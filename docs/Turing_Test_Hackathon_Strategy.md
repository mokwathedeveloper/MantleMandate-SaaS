
# Turing Test Hackathon 2026 Strategy for MantleMandate-SaaS

### **Phase 1: ClawHack (AI Agent Deployment)**

#### **Key Actions:**
1. **Deploy AI Agents**: 
   - Develop and deploy **AI agents** that execute on-chain strategies within **Mantle's DeFi ecosystem**.
   - The agents must be evaluated on **trading volume** and **ROI**.

2. **Key Focus Areas**:
   - The AI agents should interact with **Merchant Moe**, **Agni Finance**, and **Fluxion** for trading. Ensure **performance transparency** and **verifiable results** on the **Mantle blockchain**.
   - Optimize the **AI agent performance** and test under real-world scenarios.

3. **On-Chain Performance Tracking**:
   - Ensure every **decision** and **outcome** by the AI agent is recorded on **Mantle’s blockchain** for transparency.

4. **Submit Open-Source Repository**:
   - Provide a **public GitHub repo** with source code, a **running demo**, and **clear documentation**.

---

### **Phase 2: AI Awakening (Human vs. AI Challenge)**

#### **Key Actions:**
1. **Track Selection**:
   - Choose a track based on your agent's **functionality** (e.g., **AI Trading & Strategy**).
   - Your project must be deployed on **Mantle Network** with **smart contracts**.

2. **Agent Identity and Performance**:
   - Each AI agent is issued a **unique identity NFT** via **ERC-8004**. Ensure the **agent's performance** is recorded and verifiable.

3. **Human vs AI Challenge**:
   - Showcase how your AI agent can **outperform** a human competitor by adapting in real-time to market changes.

4. **AI Trading Strategy / Alpha & Data**:
   - Ensure your solution uses **AI to generate Alpha** from **on-chain data**.

---

### **Key Hackathon Judging Criteria:**
- **Technical Depth (30%)**: Your AI system should be **robust**, **efficient**, and **integrated seamlessly** with **Mantle’s DeFi ecosystem**.
- **Innovation (25%)**: Your AI solution should bring **new paradigms** to Web3.
- **Mantle Ecosystem Contribution (25%)**: Make **substantive use** of **Mantle’s network**.
- **Product Completeness (20%)**: Ensure a **running demo**, **user experience**, and **scalability**.

---

### **Final Steps for Winning**:

1. **Technical Deployment**: 
   - Ensure your **smart contracts** are deployed on **Mantle Mainnet** or **Testnet**.
   - **Verify** contracts on **Mantle Explorer**.

2. **Product Completeness**:
   - **Publicly accessible frontend demo**.
   - **Demo video** (≥ 2 minutes) showcasing core use cases.

3. **Documentation**:
   - Provide a **well-documented GitHub repo** with **README** (setup, architecture overview, deployed contract address).
   - Include an explanation of how your **AI platform** interacts with **Mantle’s blockchain**.

4. **UI/UX**: Ensure a **user-friendly** platform with consistent design, smooth interactions, and AI capabilities presented clearly. The **Best UI/UX Award** will judge your design.

5. **Community Engagement**:
   - Engage with **DoraHacks** and encourage votes for community recognition.
